Celestial Church of Christ History

The **Celestial Church of Christ (C.C.C.)** is a spiritual, world-wide African Initiated Church founded on **October 4, 1947**, in Porto-Novo, Republic of Benin, by **Rev. Prophet Pastor Founder Samuel Biléhou Joseph Oshoffa**.

According to church history, Papa Oshoffa was lost in the wilderness of Benin for several months, where he received divine spiritual commissions to establish a church that brings mankind closer to the path of light, healing, and angelic liturgical structures.

Known for its distinctive white robes (Soutana), barefoot compliance in holy grounds, and deep angelic chants, C.C.C. has grown to encompass millions of members across several continents, with its Supreme International Headquarters dynamically centered in **Imeko, Ogun State, Nigeria**.
